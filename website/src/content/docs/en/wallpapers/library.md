title: Wallpaper library
description: Browse, search, sort, filter, and favorite your live wallpapers in the "Installed" tab.
---

The "Installed" tab is your local wallpaper library, showing every wallpaper you've imported or downloaded.

## Browsing

Wallpapers appear as a grid of cards, each showing a preview image and a name. Scroll to browse everything, and click a card to select it and load the preview and properties panel.

## Search

The search box on the left of the toolbar filters the library by keyword (such as a name) in real time.

## Sorting

The right side of the toolbar controls sorting:

- **Sort by**: name, rating, or file size.
- **Sort direction**: ascending or descending; click the direction button to toggle.

## Filtering

Click "Filter" to expand the sidebar, where you can combine the following conditions (for the detailed options, see the [Interface tour](/en/guides/interface/)):

- **Show only**: highly rated, my favorites, mobile compatible, audio responsive, customizable.
- **Type**: scene, video, web, application, preset.
- **Content rating**: everyone, mild nudity, mature.
- **Tags**: Abstract, Anime, Game, Landscape, Nature, Sci-Fi, and other common Wallpaper Engine categories.

The "Reset filters" button at the top of the sidebar clears all conditions at once.

## Favorites

Once you add a wallpaper to "My favorites", you can check "My favorites" in the filter panel to quickly find the works you use most.

## Refresh

When "Auto refresh" is enabled in [Settings](/en/settings/general/), a refresh button appears in the toolbar to re-scan the wallpaper directory and promptly reflect newly imported or downloaded content.

## Wallpaper sources

The library brings together works from different sources:

- [Imported local directories](/en/formats/import/)
- [Wallpaper packages generated by video conversion](/en/formats/video-convert/)
- [Content downloaded from the Steam Workshop](/en/workshop/download/)

For where each source is actually stored, see [Data directories](/en/advanced/data-directories/).

Once you've picked a wallpaper, head to [Apply wallpaper](/en/wallpapers/apply/) to set it as your desktop wallpaper.
